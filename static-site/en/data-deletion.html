Data Deletion Request

Your privacy is important to us. You have the right to request deletion of your personal data from our applications and services.

How to Request Data Deletion

If you would like to delete your account and all associated personal data from our applications (including POZZ), please follow these steps:

1

Send an Email

Send an email to hello@alicantilki.com with the subject line "Data Deletion Request"

2

Provide Identification

Include the email address or user ID associated with your account to help us identify your data

3

Confirmation

We will confirm your identity and process your request within 30 days as required by GDPR and other privacy regulations

What Data Will Be Deleted?

Upon receiving your data deletion request, we will permanently delete:

  • Your account information (name, email, profile data)
  • Any content you have created or uploaded
  • Usage data and analytics associated with your account
  • Any other personally identifiable information we have collected

Important Notes

Data deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Once your data is deleted, you will not be able to recover your account or any associated information.

We may retain certain information as required by law or for legitimate business purposes (such as fraud prevention), but all personal identifiers will be removed.

The deletion process typically takes up to 30 days to complete. You will receive a confirmation email once the process is finished.

Need Help?

If you have any questions about the data deletion process or our privacy practices, please don't hesitate to contact us at hello@alicantilki.com.
