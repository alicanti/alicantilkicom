Privacy Policy

POZZ - AI Pose Generation App

Last Updated: January 2025

Your Privacy Matters: This Privacy Policy explains how POZZ collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our AI pose generation application.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Anonymous authentication tokens through Firebase Auth
  • User Preferences: Onboarding selections, style preferences, and app settings
  • Gender Information: Optional gender selection for personalized pose recommendations

1.2 Images and Content

  • Uploaded Images: Selfie photos you choose to transform using POZZ
  • Face Data: Facial images and facial feature data contained within uploaded photos, collected with your explicit consent for AI-powered pose generation including style transformations, celebrity looks, historical figures, and other user-requested pose modifications
  • Generated Images: AI-processed results from your pose generation sessions
  • Text Prompts: AI prompts used for pose generation

1.3 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device information (IDFV, model, operating system version)
  • App usage statistics and analytics through Firebase Analytics
  • Crash reports and error logs
  • Performance metrics and generation success rates

1.4 Purchase Information

  • In-app purchase records managed by RevenueCat
  • Credit balance and usage history
  • Subscription status and renewal information
  • Subscription packages (Weekly, Monthly, Annual Pro plans)
  • Credit package purchases (Small, Medium, Large, Mega packs)

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Primary Services

  • Pose Generation: To perform AI-powered pose transformation and generation
  • Facial Processing: To process face data for pose generation features such as celebrity style transformations, historical figure poses, fashion looks, and other pose modifications requested by users
  • Account Management: To maintain your account and preferences
  • Credit System: To track and manage your credit usage and purchases
  • Personalization: To provide customized pose recommendations based on your preferences

2.2 Improvement and Support

  • Analyze app performance and pose generation success rates
  • Improve AI model accuracy and pose quality
  • Provide customer support and troubleshooting
  • Develop new pose categories and features

3. Data Storage and Processing

3.1 Image Processing

Important: Your images are processed by third-party AI services (beautykit.bivex.com) to provide pose generation capabilities. Images are transmitted securely and processed according to our data protection standards.

3.2 Cloud Storage

  • Firebase Storage: Temporary storage of uploaded images and face data during processing (maximum 24 hours)
  • Firebase Firestore: Account data, generated pose history, and app preferences
  • Local Device: App cache and generated pose results

3.3 Data Retention

  • Face Data and Images: All uploaded images containing face data are automatically deleted from our servers within 24 hours of upload
  • Generated Images: Stored locally on your device and optionally in your personal Firebase storage
  • Account Data: Retained while your account is active
  • Analytics Data: Non-personal analytics data retained for up to 2 years for service improvement

4. Third-Party Services

4.1 Firebase (Google)

  • Purpose: Authentication, database, analytics, and cloud storage
  • Data Shared: Account information, app usage data, generated pose history
  • Privacy Policy: Google Privacy Policy

4.2 RevenueCat

  • Purpose: In-app purchase management and subscription handling
  • Data Shared: Purchase information, user ID, subscription status
  • Privacy Policy: RevenueCat Privacy Policy

4.3 AI Processing Services

  • Purpose: Pose generation and facial transformation
  • Data Shared: Images (including face data) and pose prompts for processing only
  • Face Data Policy: Face data is NOT shared with third parties for any purpose other than immediate pose generation
  • Security: All data transmission is encrypted and face data is automatically deleted within 24 hours

5. Data Security

5.1 Encryption

  • All data transmission uses industry-standard encryption (TLS/SSL)
  • Sensitive data is encrypted at rest in Firebase
  • Images are processed through secure, encrypted channels

5.2 Access Control

  • Limited access to personal data on a need-to-know basis
  • Regular security audits and monitoring
  • Secure authentication for all system access

6. Your Privacy Rights

6.1 Access and Control

  • View Your Data: Access your account information and pose generation history
  • Update Information: Modify your account preferences and settings
  • Download Data: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Delete Account: Remove your account and associated data through the app

6.2 Communication Preferences

  • Control notifications and app communications
  • Opt out of analytics data collection (where legally permitted)
  • Manage marketing communications preferences

7. Face Data Collection and Processing

7.1 Face Data Collection

POZZ collects facial images and facial feature data contained within photos that you voluntarily upload. This collection occurs only with your explicit consent when you choose to use pose generation features.

7.2 Purpose of Face Data Processing

Face data is used exclusively for:

  • Creating celebrity-style pose transformations
  • Generating fashion and lifestyle poses
  • Historical figure and artistic style poses
  • Travel and adventure pose styles
  • Professional and business pose generation
  • Other user-requested pose transformations

7.3 Face Data Sharing and Storage

Third-Party Sharing: Face data is NOT shared with any third parties for any purpose other than immediate pose generation processing.
  • Storage Location: Face data is temporarily stored in Firebase Storage during processing
  • Retention Period: All face data is automatically deleted within 24 hours of upload

7.4 User Control

You maintain full control over your face data. You can delete uploaded images and generated poses at any time through the app, and all face data is automatically removed from our servers within 24 hours regardless of user action.

8. Children's Privacy

POZZ is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover that we have collected information from a child under 13, we will delete that information immediately.

9. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed and stored in countries other than your own, including the United States. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data in accordance with this Privacy Policy and applicable laws.

10. Data Breaches

In the unlikely event of a data breach that affects your personal information, we will:

  • Notify you promptly as required by law
  • Take immediate steps to secure the affected systems
  • Cooperate with relevant authorities
  • Provide updates on our investigation and remediation efforts
